Welcome to Multimodal RAG Document Analyzer!

This application parses documents segmentally and uses Gemini Vision models to convert diagrams, charts, and tables into descriptive text for retrieval.

To start, upload a PDF containing charts/text (e.g., quarterly financial statements or scientific papers) and ask questions like:

  • "What are the quarterly revenues shown in the bar chart?"
  • "Explain the trend illustrated in the page 3 chart."
  • "Summarize the structured table showing expenses."
